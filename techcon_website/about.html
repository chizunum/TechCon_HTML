History of TechCon IBM TechCon, formerly known as the IBM Technical Interchange, is a multi-day technical conference focused on IBM's latest technologies, products, and services. It serves as a platform for IBM developers, clients, and partners to learn, collaborate, and innovate around IBM's offerings. The conference typically features keynotes from IBM executives, technical sessions led by IBM experts, hands-on labs, and opportunities to network with peers and industry leaders.
Mission to connect, educate, and empower users of IBM technology
